# Behavioral & Leadership Interviews

## Overview

Section **20**.

## STAR Format

| Letter | Content |
|--------|---------|
| **S** | Situation — 2 sentences |
| **T** | Task — your responsibility |
| **A** | Action — what **you** did |
| **R** | Result — metrics, learning |

## AI-Specific Scenarios

**Q: Tell me about shipping an LLM feature to production.**

> Cover: eval harness, canary, monitoring faithfulness, rollback story.

**Q: Disagreement on RAG vs fine-tuning.**

> Show data-driven eval comparison; respect dissent; experiment.

**Q: Model behaved unsafely in staging.**

> Halt launch; red-team case; guardrails; postmortem.

**Q: Mentoring junior on agents.**

> Pair on tool schema; code review traces; golden tasks.

## Leadership (Staff+)

- Cross-team platform vs product delivery tradeoff
- Setting eval standards org-wide
- Prioritizing tech debt in prompt pipelines

## Common Mistakes

- Team "we" without your role
- No measurable result
- Blaming model vendor without ownership

## Preparation

Prepare 5 stories: success, failure, conflict, leadership, learning.
